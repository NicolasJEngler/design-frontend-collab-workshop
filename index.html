In this workshop we're going to go through the advantages and caveats of implementing UI/UX designs on the front-end side of things while respecting the creative proposals and direction of designers.

What do designers and front-developers do similarly?

To begin this workshop we need to understand what we, designers and front-end devs do similarly. This exercise will help us get the bigger picture when it comes to going through workflows that while different at first glance have a lot in common.

The main idea is to understand what first-level limitations we have and how to break through those limitations to go through what I call n-level limitations with the resources we have.
